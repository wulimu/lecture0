This page introduces the basics of waste water treatment process. It includes the following parts:

  1. Individual Sewage Collection;
  2. Sewage Transportation;
  3. Waste Water Treatment Plant (WWTP).

Waste Water Treatment Plant is where all sewage water gets treated to a certain standard that it will discharge back into the surface water system. A complete WWTP normally includes these units:

  • Primary Screen;
  • Oxidation Ditch (Aerator);
  • Secondary Clarifier;
  • Tertiary Filter System;
  • Chlorine Contact Chamber;
  • R.A.S./W.A.S. Pump Station;
  • Digester;
  • Belt Press System;
